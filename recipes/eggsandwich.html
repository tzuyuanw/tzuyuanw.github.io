Boiled Egg Sandwich

Having pancakes for breakfast is a wonderful thing...

This recipe is easy to remember and allows you to make your own pancake from scratch. After I started using this recipe, I never bought another box of pancake mix. This recipe originally came from Jamie Oliver's Foodtube recipes on YouTube.

Ingredients:

  • 1 to 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Toast
  • 1 Pickle
  • Butter to Spread on Toast

Steps:

  1. Add tap cold water into a pot and add in the eggs.
  2. Turn the stove on to medium high heat and cook until the water boils.
  3. When the water boils turn the heat off, then keep the eggs in the pot with the lid on.
  4. While you are waiting for the egg, heat up your toast.
  5. While you are waiting for the toast, slice the pickle.
