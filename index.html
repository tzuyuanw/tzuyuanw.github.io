about me

I am a recent graduate from the Master of City Planning program at the University of Pennsylvania Weitzman School of Design, focusing on transportation planning and urban design. As a master student, I also worked as a research assistant working on projects related to transportation planning. My work involves working with data such as ridership numbers, household travel surveys, and other demographic data. I also work on visualizing data using graphics and maps.
