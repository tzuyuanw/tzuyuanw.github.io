This page presents a collection of graphics I made as a graduate student and a research assistant. These graphics were made using a combination of ArcGIS, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop.

Workshop

In workshop, I was responsible for preparing a summary of the existing condition on transportation as well as creating graphics for proposed interventions relating to transportation in the community.

Fare Capping
This graphic was created to explain the concept of fare capping. A fare system with this mechanism caps the total amount of payment so that frequent riders pay the same amount per month or week as people who purchased a pass. This allows frequent riders who could not pay for a monthly or weekly pass to also benefit from the reduced cost of riding transit.
